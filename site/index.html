Each valid gap is measured by its merit: gap / ln(p). Higher merit means a rarer, more mathematically significant discovery.

Record-worthy gaps discovered by miners join the global prime gap database maintained by mathematicians worldwide.

In Memory of Jonnie Frey

1989 – 2017

Jonnie Frey created Gapcoin, the first cryptocurrency whose Proof-of-Work advanced prime gap research. He proved that the energy spent securing a blockchain could simultaneously contribute to human knowledge.

Freycoin carries his vision forward.
